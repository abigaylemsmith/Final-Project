"Most of the time, I feel like a scribble. Formless and incomprehensible in nature. My mind a stream of illegible thoughts."
...

Through my work, I strive to convey the innerworkings of my mind. An attempt at capturing the collection of conversations, experiences, and emotions that have resided soley within my body. I wish to illustrate my perception of the world by authentically displaying what I can of my soul, and to invite others to do the same.
